About PetMalu

PetMalu is a professional grooming studio based in Leon, Iloilo. We provide gentle, high-quality grooming for dogs and cats — from baths and trims to nail care and ear cleaning. Our team prioritizes animal comfort and safety, using only veterinarian-approved products and modern equipment.

  • Experienced Groomers — trained in breed-specific cuts and stress-minimizing handling.
  • Clean & Safe Space — regular sanitation, separate areas for bathing & drying.
  • Pet-First Philosophy — low-force restraint, breaks on request, and lots of treats.
